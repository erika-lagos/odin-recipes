Tiramisu

Description

Tiramisu is a traditional italian dessert made with mascarpone cheese, coffee, and chocolate

Ingredients

  • Mascarpone cheese
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Marsala wine
  • Espresso coffee
  • Ladyfingers

Steps

  1. Whip mascarpone, marsala, sugar and eggs until fluffy
  2. One by one, dip the ladyfingers into the espresso
  3. Working in layers, add a base of espresso-dipped ladyfingers on a serving dish
  4. Follow the base layer with about 1/3rd of the mascarpone mixture
  5. Continue alternating the two, until you add the last portion of mascarpone mixture
  6. Top with shaved chocolate and refrigerate for at least 2 hours

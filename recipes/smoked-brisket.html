Smoked Brisket

Description

Brisket is a delicious and succulent cut of meat that becomes very tender when cooked slowly.

Here, we will show you how to use a smoker to cook a whole brisket.

Ingredients

  • One whole (approx 5 lbs) beef brisket, trimmed
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Apple cider vinegar or apple juice

Steps

  1. First, trim your brisket of excess fat
  2. Once trimmed, cover your brisket with equal amounts of salt and pepper
  3. Smoke at 225-250 F for 8-12 hrs. Timing will depend on the size of your brisket
  4. After hour 5, spritz with apple juice or vinegar every 30 minutes
  5. When the internal temperature reaches 190 F, allow to rest in an insulated cooler for 1 hr before serving.
